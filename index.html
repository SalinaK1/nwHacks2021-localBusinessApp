WHY WE MADE LOCAL

Local Businesses have been hit hard during this pandemic. The time has been testing and overwhelming. We, at LOCAL, aim at combining the best of humanity with modern technologies and creativity. We connect local businesses with people around them to keep their business running and serving customers the safety of shopping from home, the authenticity of a local business and making the communiy better, one order at a time.

Why Local matters more than anything right now


According to Yelp, in the year 2020 from March 1 until August 31, 163,735 businesses have been closed in the U.S due to COVID-19. The number is staggering and when we think about the whole world, local businesses need the support of community more than anything in these unprecedented times. Hence, we created this website as a means to connect local businesses with people around them.

How does it help?

  • The safety that consumer gets while shopping at home is unmatched to any other modes of shopping.
  • The local businesses can even get orders from people who might have not known they existed but the website showcases each and every business to be brought into the public eye.
  • The consumers do not have to look far away from their homes and shop from location nearby them, thanks to our Location feature which helps you connect within the area you need.
  • It helps keep local businesses running and they even can scale to larger neighbourhoods once they have the capacity.

So, come join us in helping local businesses in keeping their livelihoods running and showcasing the best of humanity!
